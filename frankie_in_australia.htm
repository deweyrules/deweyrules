Frankie In Australia!

Yes, Frankie came to visit! Although it was only a short visit he will be back!

The main reason for his visit was to present an award (Best Comedy Program) for the 2002 Logies!

And, according to smh.com.au Frankie 'thought the Logies were better than the Oscars and the Golden Globes. "It's a lot better organised," he said.'

And at the after parties, being a good 16 year old boy, Frankie stuck to water and lemonade!

However, while he was here he did a few interviews!
